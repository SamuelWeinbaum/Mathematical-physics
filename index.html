Mathematical Approach to QM

Reading Group – Summer 2025

What

This is the webpage for the Summer 2025 Reading Group on the math behind quantum mechanics, organized by McGill students. We will be working through the lecture notes:

“Lecture Notes on Hilbert Spaces and Quantum Mechanics” by N.P. Landsman.

The goal is for this to be low effort, casual, and informal. Every week, one of us would present a section of the notes (as in give a lecture), and we would take turns doing so.

We meet twice a week, every Tuesday and Thursday, from 4 PM to 5 PM in BURN 1234.

To give a talk, or simply for more information email QM.with-real-math@mcgill.ca. (This isn't the real email click on the link.)
