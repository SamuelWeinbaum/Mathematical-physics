What

This is the webpage for the Fall 2025 Reading Group on *The Large-Scale Structure of Spacetime* by Hawking & Ellis. We are McGill students studying the book as part of an independent study with Prof. A.

We meet twice weekly (Mondays and Fridays). One of us will present (rotating) and anyone is welcome to join. Before each meeting, we will post the topic here.
